About Me

Hi there! My name is Joy Gilmore. I am a multi-disciplinary freelance graphic designer, working with all different types of clients. My specialty is print, campaign design, and brand & identity design. I love working with new clients and I am always willing to take on new projects!

A little bit about my history ..... I started my career as a freelance designer in 2002, when I was attending the Herron School of Art and Design. I created projects for clients including: logos, posters, flyer, and other types of design. I really enjoyed doing this and graduated in 2008 with my Bachelor’s in Visual Communication Design. From then on I worked for many companies doing packaging design, branding, campaign design, healthcare design and much more!
