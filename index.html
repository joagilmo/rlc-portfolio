HELLO!

Nice to meet you! I'm Joy. I am a multi-disciplinary graphic designer with a BFA in Visual Communication Design from the Herron School of Art. I have been designing for over 8 years and absolutely love it. I have had the pleasure of working with all different types of clients. My specialty is print design, campaign design, and brand & identity design. I love working with new clients and I am always willing to take on new projects.
